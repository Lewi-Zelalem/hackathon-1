About Us

Inovations Coming To Life

Welcome to FlipHacks, where every event tells a story of passion, tradition, and themes that have stood the test of time.
At FlipHacks, we organize school-hosted hackathon camps wich usually take around 24 - 48hours and offers several benefits: a sense of accomplishment, new technical skills, exposure to cutting-edge technologies, networking opportunities, and improved problem-solving abilities. These events foster creativity, teamwork, and resilience, making them valuable experiences for students. 🚀

Join us on this gastronomic journey as we continue to craft stories, one event at a time.
Thank you for being a part of our story.
